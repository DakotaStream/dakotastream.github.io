How to check out a design station.

  1. Print your class schedule showing EE-496 enrolment.

  2. Fill in the required information on the EE-496 locker checkout form and print it.  Please type in the text boxes on form, do not fill out with a pen or pencil.

  3. Bring the EE-496 form to your sponsoring faculty member for a signature.

  4. Two types of senior design lockers are available: Large lockers in  rooms C-248, 249, 251, 252 and limited availability private design stations in A308.
    Most projects are assigned to the large lockers. A detailed description of your project requirements will help you get the right locker and equipment.

  5. Bring your signed EE-496 form, your class schedule , and a copy of your school ID  to Mike Obermeyer Room C-250.
    If no one is available, place the paperwork under the door.
    Your locker number and combination will be sent via email.

  6. Upon receipt of your locker assignment, you will need access to the room where your project is located.  Click here for access.
